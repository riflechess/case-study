Usage
lfs will provide a json-formatted list of files, their size (in bytes) from a specified directory/filesystem.

Use the links below, or use a REST client GET /lfs.php?fs=[filesystem]

Examples
lfs /var
lfs /usr
lfs /etc
lfs /bin
lfs --help



curl "https://cs.sitsev.net/lfs.php?fs=/var" > var.json
